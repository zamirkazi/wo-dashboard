## Asset Drill-Down — The Main Tool

The most useful section for property managers. Select your property from the dropdown and get:

### 📈 Trends Tab
- Monthly average completion days (is your property getting faster or slower?)
- % of WOs completed within 24 hours, by month
- Monthly volume (how many WOs are being submitted?)
- Speed distribution bar showing the exact split between same-day, 1–2 day, and 3+ day closes

### 📂 Categories Tab
Charts and a full table showing how your property performs across each work order category (Appliances, Plumbing, HVAC, Doors & Locks, Electrical, etc.):
- Volume per category
- Average days to close
- % closed within 24 hours
- Number of slow WOs (7+ days) per category

### 🔧 Subcategories Tab
The most granular view — every specific repair type your property has on record:
- **Plumbing:** Toilet, Tub/Shower, Sink, Water Heater, Garbage Disposal, Leak/Water Damage
- **Appliances:** Washer, Dryer, Dishwasher, Stove/Oven, Refrigerator
- **HVAC:** AC/HVAC, Heater, Thermostat, Vents/Ducts
- **Doors & Locks:** Entry Door, Patio Door, Interior Door, Mailbox/Keys, Lock
- **Electrical:** Lighting/Fan, Outlet/Switch, Smoke/CO Detector, Circuit Breaker
- Full table with avg days, % in 24h, slow WO count, and a letter grade (A–F)

### ⚡ Speed Analysis Tab
- Your property's completion speed vs the portfolio average (3.12 days)
- Month-by-month trend line overlaid against the portfolio benchmark
- How your property's speed distribution compares to portfolio norms

### 🚨 Priority Tab
- How fast Emergency, High, Medium, and Low priority WOs are being closed
- Are high-priority WOs actually being treated as urgent?

### 🔍 Recurring Issues Tab
- Top 10 keywords appearing in work order descriptions (leak, clog, ac, door, etc.)
- Categories with the most chronic slow WOs
- Written assessment of your property's main maintenance gaps

### ⚠️ Safety Tab
- Complete mold/mildew WO log with exact dates and days-to-resolve
- Safety checklist: mold status, slow WO audit, response time rating, same-day close rate
- Specific action items flagged for your property

## Compare Page — 4 Ways to View

Pick any subcategory (AC/HVAC, Toilet, Washer, Stove/Oven, etc.) and switch between four view modes:

**📊 Bar Charts** — Classic side-by-side bars. Best for a quick visual comparison.

**📋 Data Table** — Clean ranked table with exact numbers, how far each property is above or below the category average, and an A–F letter grade. Best for sharing in reports.

**🕸 Radar Chart** — Spider/web chart showing top properties across 5 dimensions at once (speed, same-day rate, volume, consistency, no-slow-WOs). A large, symmetrical shape = well-rounded performer. Lopsided = strong in one area, weak in others.

**🌡 Heat Map** — All properties × all subcategories in a single color-coded grid. Green = fast, Red = slow. Best for spotting patterns — if an entire column is red, that subcategory has a portfolio-wide problem.

## Understanding the Numbers

### Average Days
The mean time from when a work order was submitted to when it was completed.

| Range | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🟢 Under 2.0 days | Strong performance |
| 🟡 2.0–3.5 days | Acceptable, room to improve |
| 🔴 Over 3.5 days | Needs attention |
| Portfolio average | 3.12 days |

### % Completed Within 24 Hours
The share of work orders that close the same day they're submitted. This is the metric **residents actually feel** — they don't see your average, they feel whether their issue was fixed today or not.

| Range | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🟢 65%+ | Great same-day performance |
| 🟡 45–65% | Average |
| 🔴 Under 45% | Most residents waiting more than a day |
| Portfolio average | 53.5% |

### Slow WOs (7+ Days)
Work orders that take more than a week to close. Even if your average looks fine, a handful of 30-day WOs drag the number up and create very unhappy residents.

| Range | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🟢 0–2% of total | Normal |
| 🟡 3–8% | Monitor closely |
| 🔴 Over 8% | Significant backlog issue |

## Tips for Property Managers

**Start with your property's Subcategories tab.** Sort by "Slow WOs (7+d)" — that column tells you exactly which repair types are creating the worst resident experiences.

**Use the Priority tab to check triage.** If your High priority WOs are taking longer than Medium, your team's prioritization process needs a review.

**Check the Recurring Issues tab keywords.** If "leak" appears 40+ times, you have a plumbing infrastructure problem, not a maintenance team problem. Different solution entirely.

**Benchmark against Skye Oaks (45728).** They average 0.43 days and close 88% within 24 hours — zero WOs over 7 days. Same portfolio, very different execution. Use their numbers as your target.

## Tips for Leadership

**Use the Scorecard** for your weekly ops review. Sort by Slow WOs column to immediately identify properties needing intervention.

**Use the Compare → Heat Map** for portfolio-wide subcategory issues. If a whole column is red across multiple properties, it's a vendor/procurement problem — not a per-property staffing conversation.

**The Mold & Safety page is compliance-critical.** Review it monthly. Any WO over 7 days to resolution needs documentation.

**The Make Ready tab shows vacancy cost.** At $50/day in lost rent, Nightingale on 25th averaging 9.6 days per make-ready costs ~$480/unit turn in vacancy.
