📣 Review Improvement Action Plan

Based on actual WO data — ranked by resident impact and review likelihood

What's Hurting Reviews Right Now
🔴 Washer/Dryer downtime — 5.25 avg days. Laundry is a daily necessity. Residents without it write reviews that week.
🔴 Dishwasher at 6.41 days — slowest appliance subcategory. 24 slow WOs portfolio-wide.
🔴 Tub/Shower at 4.23 days — 31 slow WOs. Can't use your shower for 7+ days = guaranteed negative review.
🔴 Patio doors at 6.57 days — security issue. Residents feel unsafe with a broken patio latch.
🔴 9 unresolved mold WOs at The Julia — no completion date on any of them.
🟡 Low priority WOs ignored for 5+ days — residents don't know their WO is "low priority." They just see nothing happening.
What's Earning You Good Reviews
✅ AC/HVAC at 1.71 days — 65% same-day. Best possible impression in summer and winter.
✅ Pest Control at 1.13 days — 81% same-day. Residents feel heard when bug/pest issues close fast.
✅ Heater at 1.43 days — critical comfort item handled well.
✅ Skye Oaks overall — 88% same-day. Every resident there is getting 5-star maintenance.
✅ Feb 2026 at 1.71 avg days — improving trend means improving reviews in Q2 2026.
✅ Smoke/CO detector response — 60.6% same-day. Residents notice when safety issues are taken seriously.
7 Highest-Impact Actions — Ranked
🔴 Do First
Resolve The Julia's 9 Open Mold WOs
9 "Mold / Mildew Issue" WOs with zero completion dates. Confirm with The Julia's team whether these are resolved. If not, this is an active habitability issue that will generate both reviews and legal exposure. Verify and document today.
🔴 Do First
Set a 24h SLA for Washers, Tubs & Patio Doors
Washers (5.25d), Tub/Shower (4.23d), and Patio Doors (6.57d) are the three subcategories most likely to generate 1-star reviews. These are daily-use or security items. Create a dedicated morning route for these three categories before any other WOs are dispatched.
🟡 High Impact
Text Residents on Every WO Open Past 24h
46.5% of WOs don't close same-day. A single "We have your request — waiting on [part/vendor], expected [date]" message turns a frustrated resident into an understanding one. This costs nothing and is the single highest-ROI communication change you can make. Silence reads as abandonment.
🟡 High Impact
Ask for Reviews Immediately After Fast Closes
You have 2,396 same-day closes this period. That's 2,396 residents who had a positive last experience with maintenance. Trigger an automated text within 2 hours of WO close: "Glad we could help! If you have 60 seconds, a review on Google helps others find us." Even 3% conversion = 72 new positive reviews.
🔵 Strategic
Export Skye Oaks's Process to Hanley Place and 45727
Same portfolio. 45728 closes 88% in 24h. 45729 closes 32% in 24h. That gap is entirely operational. Interview the 45728 maintenance team: staffing ratio, vendor contact list, dispatch priority order, morning routine. Run a 60-day process transplant at 45729. That property alone has 599 WOs — fixing it moves your entire portfolio average.
🔵 Strategic
Preventative Plumbing Audit at Top 3 Leak Properties
The Boardwalk (leak×79), Hanley Place (leak×58), Upland Townhomes (leak×39) — 176 leak WOs combined. Recurring leaks mean the same residents are reporting the same issues repeatedly. A one-time plumbing walkthrough (supply lines, shutoff valves, drain condition) could eliminate 30%+ of repeat WOs and prevent the "this keeps happening" reviews.
🟢 Use Your Wins
Use HVAC Speed as a Renewal & Marketing Talking Point
Your HVAC team closes 65% of AC and Heating tickets within 24 hours. This is a genuine, data-backed differentiator. Put it in renewal letters: "When your AC stops working, we respond in under 24 hours in most cases." Put it on your listing page. Most competitors can't say this with data. You can.
